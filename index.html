About IIITB

Set up in the year 1999, and promoted by the Karnataka government and the IT industry, IIIT-B attracts quality students every year from all over India and abroad to its acclaimed M.Tech, Master of Science by research, M.Sc in Digital Society and Ph.D programs. The institute has a short yet distinguished history of producing high quality post-graduate engineers every year. The 2000+ strong alumni occupy key positions in a number of corporations, delivering tremendous value to their organizations. Students from different technical and cultural backgrounds come together in the institute, realizing the potential of unity and team work. Immense support from the wonderful faculty enables the students to stay on the path of scientific enquiry and together, they have produced several patents, published in influential journals and conferences and carried out innovative projects. And to think we are just 16 years old...

Life IITB

If one word can describe life at IIIT-B, it would be “dynamic”. Students at IIIT-B have formed various clubs and groups, and actively participate in events, both curricular, as well as extra-curricular. We strongly believe in overall development of the body as well as the soul, and all resources of the college are used to ensure the same. From regular movie nights organised by the Multimedia club, to the electronic system design workshops conducted by the college, we try to ensure everyone’s interests are covered. AIKYAM, our social and community outreach has played a leading role in helping poor children build a happy bright future, while the Environment Club ensures that we give back to nature.

Academic Infrastucture

Designed and built in compliance with the highest global standards, the teaching and learning infrastructure features the most advanced elements of contemporary academic tools. With addition of a new floor to the main academic block, the institute now has over 120,000 square feet of air-conditioned space, uninterrupted power supply, and a wellcrafted interior, the Institute offers a world-class environment for students and faculty. All the classrooms are “smart,” with high-speed data networks and large projection systems for audio and video. The well-designed main classroom comfortably seats in excess of 150 students. Video conferencing capabilities are built in using state-of-the-art audio-visual equipment. They include electronic smart boards, location-sensing microphones, and multiple LCD projectors,thus enabling an enriching learning experience. The Institute has an academic library with an excellent collection of books,journals, and magazines both in print and electronic form. Students have privileged free access to numerous online research resources (such as the ACM Digital Library, IEEEXplore, JSTOR).
